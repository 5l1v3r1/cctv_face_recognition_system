IP CCTV Face Recognition System: Training New Face

Taking Pictures

  1. Use a good mobile / compact camera with a resolution of 12 Megapixels or above.

  2. Find a well lit surrounding and turn on the HDR feature of the camera.

  3. Take 8 photos of the person’s face; with the face (eyes, nose, mouth) clearly visible: Left, Right , Top, Bottom, Top_Left, Top_Right, Bottom_Left and Bottom_Right.

  4. Crop the photos to only show the face.

  5. Copy the cropped photos and convert the copied photos into Black and White (for night mode detection).

Saving the Pictures

  • Save the photos in an organized structure (i.e. separate folders for each person).

  • The file name for each photo can be like: first 3-4 letters of person’s first name and first 2 letters of person’s last name.

  • Keep photos of known faces in a single folder.

Updating the CSV File
